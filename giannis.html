Giannis Antetokounmpo

Giannis Antetokounmpo was born December 6, 1994 ( 24 years) . Giannis was born in Athens Greece where he used to sell sun glasses and dvds to help provide for his family . His father had died of a heart attack in 2017 and 2 years later, Giannis went on to go an win Most Valuble Player of the Nba in 2018-2019 nba season. In his speech he mentioned he did it for his father and how he misses him and the way his father pushed to be how great he is today .
